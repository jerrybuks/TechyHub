About Us

TechyHub is an NGO, comprising of a group of young tech enthuisaits, that wants to help people get started in top notch tech carrers, that they may be scared to dive into. We structure our programmes to carry everyone along through our intensive onsight bootcamp. We believe that there are so many persons out there with the potential and talent to revolutionalize technology all over Africa and the world at Large, but do not have the necessary resources to make this happen. What we do at TechyHub is to train and empower these people, so that they can follow their dreams, actualize it and then help others actualize theirs. Our primary goal is to help people to adapt and be prepared to play important roles, as technology revolutionizes the world, so that they are not left out. Our primary goal is not profit, but rather to help people. All applicants will receive consideration for enrollment without regard to age, ancestry, color, family background, gender identity or expression, genetic information, marital status, medical condition, physical or mental disability, political affiliation, race, religion, or any other characteristic. We also consider everyone regardless of criminal histories, consistent with legal requirements.
