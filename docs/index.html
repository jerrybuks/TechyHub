Software Engineering

Our software engineering programme is designed to take you through a rigourous training in software development. The training covers all that you will need to become a professional software developer. We will also teach you how to architect and design software solutions, to solve all kinds of problems you will come across, as a software engineer. Beyond just learn how to write code, our syllabus also cover core software engineering subject matters like, algorithms, data-structures, design patterns e.t.c.

UI/UX Design

Design is a broad stream of subjects and isn’t limited to graphic design.We train people in UI/UX design. UI means user interface, while UX means user experience. UI/UX Training exists to help clients understand how user’s thoughts, feelings, and behavior intersect with user interface design. As a UX designer you will learn how to execute, facilitate and analyze research and data, while for the UI you will learn how to do composition and graphic design and have a knack for palettes, typography and branding in order to make an interface shine and have its own identity. With our rigorous UI/UX scheme and training, we will get to the point where you can deliver profressional services to clients.

Data Science

In very simple terms, Data science is the study of data. It involves developing methods of recording, storing, and analyzing data to effectively extract useful information. The goal of data science is to gain insights and knowledge from any type of data — both structured and unstructured. Data science is a relatively new field, but with great potentials to revolutionalize the way, alot of problems are solved. Our Data science curriculum is designed to teach people from begginer level to a profressional standard.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the protection of internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from cyberattacks. It has to with the techniques of protecting computers, networks, programs and data from unauthorized access or attacks that are aimed for exploitation. Our cybersecurity training is designed ad structured by someone with quality industrial experience. You will not only gain theoretical knowlegde of cybersecurity, but we will also you how to put these knowledge into practise.

About Us

TechyHub is an NGO, comprising of a group of young tech enthuisaits, that wants to help people get started in top notch tech carrers, that they may be scared to dive into.
